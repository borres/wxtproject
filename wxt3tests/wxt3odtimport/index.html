More demonstration of Open Office import

Testing PI:importodf

Testing import of material from Open Office textdocuments, OpenOffice.org. This is in an experimental stage and some work remains to be done. The rawmaterial for this site is a Open Office file: doc1.odt

A section

Purpose

The idea is to give authors not familiar to XML and HTML a way of producing material for a website, or importing material from reports written in Open Office, or even material written in Microsoft Office, and converted to Open Office.

An other section

Limitations

This mechanism is not complete and free from faults. We can only deal with text, tables, images, lists and to some extent formulas.

Formulas are at the present represented by MathML, which not all browsers are too happy with.

The concept of «dpath» for selecting material from a flat documentstructure is not waterproof, but it works on simple constructs.

WXT also assume that headings are style as Heading1, Heading2 etc
