<h1>OpenGL</h1>
<p>
   OpenGL is:
</p>
<!-- list of things -->
<ul>
<li>is a library for programming of 2D and 3D graphics.</li>
<li>is established as an industry standard and reference standard.</li>
<li>is developed by Silicon Graphics.</li>
<li>has a long history under other names, GL.</li>
<li>was developed under control of an Architecture Review Board, 
which now is a part of 
<span class="ref" <a href="http://www.opengl.org/about/arb/">The Khronos Group.</a></span></li>
</ul>
<p>
 OpenGL is continuously developed, with new versions. Latest version, may 2009, is 3.1.
 One major addition to the OpenGL concept is OpenGL Shading Language. 
 OpenGL is developed as platform independent package and runs on a lot of platforms.
</p>
